Why Choose Brigade

Industry Leading AR-15 Receivers

Brigade Manufacturing is built by a team of professionals with over 150 years of combined experience to bring you a top of the line product. We are dedicated to providing you with industry leading AR-15 Battle Rifles using only the best quality upper and lower AR-15 receivers on the market. We guarantee your 100% satisfaction. Continue browsing our site and discover why you should become part of the Brigade. continue reading

Reliable

Brigade products are designed with long term reliability in mind. Brigade receivers are coated using Cerakote's industry leading finishes.

Durable

Brigade receivers are not only reliable but durable as well. Machined from a 7075-T6 Aircraft Aluminum Billet we use the latest equipment technology available.

Variety

The surface of this lower receiver is available in four different colors of Cerakote finish which is the ultimate firearms coating in the market today.

Quality

Hand bead blasted for a smooth surface finish. Our receivers are renowned for their exceptional quality.

Precision

The Brigade Manufacturing design team has created a match grade precision receivers that can be used for the ultimate build of your tactical AR Rifle.

Service

We pride ourselves on great customer service and go above and beyond to ensure our customers receive a product that is sure to please.
